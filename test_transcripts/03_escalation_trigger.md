# Escalation Trigger

Customer: I am really frustrated and want to complain about my last appointment.

AI: I do not have that information in the clinic SOP, so I should connect you with the
team rather than guess. I am handing this to a human agent now. Reason:
angry_sentiment_or_complaint, low_confidence_or_out_of_scope.

Expected result: The assistant detects angry sentiment or complaint language and logs the
handoff reason.
